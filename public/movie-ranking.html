BEST MOVIES ACCORDING TO JERIN

MY TOP 5 MOVIES

The Passion of Christ

The Crucifixion of Jesus Christ, all the torments that he had to endure for us for our SALVATION. That the sacrifice was not merely a physical pain but also emotional one where Jesus was God in human flesh. Where his own did not recognise him but handed him over to a death sentence.

Kireedam

A bright young man with high dreams and aspirations to make his father proud and is on the verge of being a police officer endeared by his father until some unforeseen circumstances causes the young man to derail and become a ruffian shattering his father's hopes and his own dreams.

The Godfather

A Son who wants to lead his own life is brought into his father's empire where he has to now handle the business and be the new Godfather. A classic movie about how u become the man u hated.

Drishyam

A man who has to protect his family after a troubled incident that leaves them at the mercy of the influential peoples. Where the man has to overcome the odds to protect his family unless which his family might go to jail.

Memories

A once good detective cop now a drunk cop who has no underlying purpose after his family's death is brought back into action after a series of incidents calls for his use of detective skills to track down the killer who has an underlying motive for his actions, a chance to redeem himself.
